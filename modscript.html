MODERATOR SCRIPT

A moderator script is a structured guide used by facilitators during usability testing to ensure consistency and neutrality across sessions. It includes a welcome, instructions, task prompts, and questions that help guide users without influencing their actions. The script maintains a natural, conversational tone to help participants feel at ease, encouraging honest feedback and allowing moderators to observe genuine user interactions with the website or product.
